This mod contains the expanded boss themes for the Beyond the Forgotten Ages mod. Simply have this mod enabled while playing BTFA and the new boss music will play automatically for supported bosses.

Acheron: "Infinite Being" by OUT
Titan Rock: "God of War" by Daisuke Ishiwatari
Arterius: "The Darkness That Chills Men's Hearts" by Oscar Araujo & Ferran Cruixent

The BTFA team does not claim to own these songs.
